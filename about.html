About Us

We are PlayPal, an online DVD distribution library. We offer a wide range of media, including songs, documentaries, and movies. Our library operates similarly to a traditional book library, with lending policies, late fees, and transaction payments. We maintain a vast collection, categorized by genre, allowing you to find your preferred discs easily. Premium stock is available, and you can place orders as per your requirements. Rest assured, your member details are kept private, and we regularly update our stock to provide you with the latest content.
